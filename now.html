Priorities

  • Enjoying the time between finishing school and beginning a dual study program at a big German automative company: Unlike many others, I'm not taking a gap year after school and will start straight into studying later this year, which creates the need to fully enjoy my free time in between. Besides some necessary university preparation, there are quite a few awesome trips and events planned - just to make sure that it doesn't get boring ;)
  • Developing a new software project that challenges my skills and expands my horizon: It's a shame to admit, but in the last year or so I really haven't started a new exciting software project with major challenges to overcome. Sure, there we some projects in the meantime, but their challenges mostly lay in the electronics department. That's why I will start a new software one in the near future and want to expand my horizon with it.
  • Exercising in the gym and becoming fitter: I'm already actively biking and running, but now also want to train in a more balanced way and build some strength. Not always easy, but I have a training schedule and am working on it.
